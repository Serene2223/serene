Our Service

Welcome to Serene, a spa designed for moms who need a break from their busy lives.

At Serene, we believe that well being, health and relaxation are essential for a happy and balanced life. That’s why we offer a range of services and treatments that cater to your specific needs and preferences.

Whether you want to be pampered by us with a facial, massage or manicure, enjoy yourself in our sauna, jacuzzi or lounge, while your kid is being watched and entertained by our professionals, or spend some quality time with your kids. We have the key to your relaxation. Serene hosts a welcoming and children friendly environment of friendly, professional, and attentive staff, as well as clean, comfortable and inviting facilities. Come and relax, recharge and replenish at Serene.

Pregnancy Massage

Our Pregnancy massage is catered to either our soon to be mothers or postpartum mothers. This type of massage offers a range of benefits such as decreasing stress levels, relieving muscle tensions, improve sleep and increase levels of “Feel-good” hormones. Our certified massage therapist will make sure you are in a comfortable position by using pillows to support your body. Gentle massaging techniques are used throughout specific areas. These techniques used by our special massage therapist are safe for both you and your baby and won’t cause any discomfort.

Childcare Services

Our rejuvenating treatment is one of our most popular facials as it helps you combat signs of aging and rejuvenates the skin. Our esthetician will firstly analyze your skin in order to determine the best approach for your skin type.
