Our Services

Welcome to Serene, a spa designed for moms who need a break from their busy lives.

At Serene, we believe that well being, health and relaxation are essential for a happy and balanced life. That’s why we offer a range of services and treatments that cater to your specific needs and preferences.

Whether you want to be pampered by us with a facial, massage or manicure, enjoy yourself in our sauna, jacuzzi or lounge, while your kid is being watched and entertained by our professionals, or spend some quality time with your kids. We have the key to your relaxation. Serene hosts a welcoming and children friendly environment of friendly, professional, and attentive staff, as well as clean, comfortable and inviting facilities. Come and relax, recharge and replenish at Serene.

Mommy Massage

You work hard as a mom, and you deserve some time for yourself. That’s why we created our Mommy Massage service, a massage that is made just for you. This massage will melt away your stress and tension, and make you feel like a new person. You will love how our massage therapist focuses on the areas that are most affected by pregnancy, childbirth and motherhood, such as your back, neck, shoulders, hips and legs. You will also enjoy the aromatherapy oils and soothing music that will transport you to a world of relaxation. Don’t miss this opportunity to treat yourself to our Mommy Massage service. You deserve it!

Duration: 60 minutes

Costs: 70 €

 Child Service

We at Serene want you to have a relaxing and enjoyable spa experience, but we also know that it is hard to find an appropriate babysitter. Because we care about you and your kids' well being and happiness, we offer a free child service that lets you indulge in your spa treatments while your kids are having a great time and learning new things in a secure and friendly setting.

Our child service is staffed by professional and experienced people who will look after your kids' needs and preferences. They will involve them in various activities such as crafts, games, stories and music. Your kids will love it while you get some peace and quiet. You can trust that your kids are in good hands at Serene.

quality time

Quality Time is a service that allows moms and their kids to bond and have fun together while learning about spa oils and massages. This service is ideal for moms who want to share their love of spa with their kids and teach them some valuable skills and knowledge. In this service, you and your kids will get to make your own spa oils using natural ingredients and aromas, and learn how to use them for massage and relaxation, as well as some basic massages that you can practice at home.

Duration: 80 minutes

Costs: 60 €
